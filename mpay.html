Experience the Future of Payments with Mi Wallet Pay

A seamless, secure, and integrated payment solution that turns points into power. Pay, recharge, and withdraw effortlessly, all from your phone.

Your Money, Your Way: Simple & Secure

Points as Currency

Mi Wallet Pay uses a simple points system where **1 point = 1 Malawian Kwacha**. This makes tracking and spending easy and transparent for everyone.

Recharge with Vouchers

Easily load points into your wallet by purchasing digital vouchers. You can buy these vouchers using your **bank card or Airtel Money**, making it convenient and accessible.

Withdraw to Airtel Money

Need to cash out? Simply request to withdraw your points, and the equivalent amount in Malawian Kwacha will be securely transferred directly to your **Airtel Money account**.

Unleash the Power of Effortless Payments

QR & NFC Payments

Pay instantly by scanning a QR code or tapping your phone using NFC.

The Fingerprint Payment Advantage

No phone? No problem. Simply use your fingerprint to pay at any store using our POS app.

For the ultimate in security and convenience, Mi Wallet Pay allows you to link your **fingerprint** to your account. This groundbreaking feature lets you make quick, secure payments without needing your phone or a card, directly through the Mi Wallet OmniPOS system.
