About Mi Wallet LTD

Empowering Malawi with seamless financial solutions since 2021.

Who We Are

Mi Wallet LTD was founded and registered in 2021 in Malawi. Based in Blantyre City, Zingwangwa Newlines, our mission is to simplify and transform the way people and businesses in Malawi manage payments. With innovation at the core, we bring secure, fast, and user-friendly digital financial solutions to empower communities and enterprises alike.
