Jump Start Guide

Master the MiWallet Credit app in 5 simple steps.

üìÑ Download Full User Manual (PDF)
Step 1
Register Your Customers

Open the app and tap the "+" (Add Customer) button. Enter their name and phone number. This creates a digital "file" for them, replacing your old notebook page.

Step 2
Giving Credit (Banja)

Tap on a customer's name to open their personal Ledger. Click "Give Credit". Enter the amount and a quick note (e.g., "2kg Sugar"). The app adds this to their total debt immediately.

Step 3
Receiving Payments

When a customer brings money, tap "Receive Money" inside their ledger. The app is smart: it automatically pays off their oldest debts first so your records stay organized.

Step 4
Check Your Dashboard

The main screen shows your "Total Amount Owing." This is your investment currently sitting with customers. It helps you know exactly how much cash you need to collect to restock.

Step 5
Backup Your Data

Before closing for the day, tap "Backup." This sends your records to our secure cloud. If you lose your phone, your money records are safe and can be restored in seconds.

üí° Pro Tip

Use the Home button to jump back to the dashboard instantly. Show the customer their "Transaction History" on the screen to build trust and avoid arguments about old payments.

Advanced Features

Powerful tools that protect your money and your business.

Multiple Credits per Customer

You can give the same customer many separate credits (for example sugar today, soap tomorrow and maize next week). Each credit is stored separately and appears clearly in their ledger.

Automatic Oldest-Debt Payments

When you receive money, MiWallet Credit automatically applies the payment to the oldest unpaid credit first. You do not need to choose which debt to clear.

Partial Payments

You can record any amount received. The system instantly updates the remaining balance and keeps the credit open until it is fully paid.

Customer Ledger

Each customer has a full transaction history showing every credit, every payment, date and running balance. This helps prevent disputes.

Automatic Backup & Sync

When internet becomes available, your data is automatically synchronized to the secure MiWallet servers. You do not lose records even if your phone is damaged.

Data Recovery (Lost Phone)

If your phone is lost, stolen or broken, simply log in on a new phone and your customers, credits and payments will be restored automatically.
